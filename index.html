about founder

Welcome! as the name suggests baked goodies we sell sweet and salty freshly baked goods for everyone and anywhere.The business started in 2010. At that time i was a college student that missed having to cook up something in the, it was my form of therapy in a way but not only that it made me miss my mothers homemade goodies when the warmth and smell filled the whole house. I love that nostalgic feeling thats why i had decided to open my own bakery with all the homemade baked goods that college students long for eversince they moved across the globe I opened it so they atleast have a place that not only reminds them of home but rather feels like it. Hope you enjoy my service and baked goods!
