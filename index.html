About me

Hi and welcome! as the name suggests baked goodies we sell sweet and freshly baked goods for everyone and anywhere. The business started in 2010.At that time i was in college and was just a student that missed having to cook up something in the kitchen as well as the taste of my moms goods. So i decided to create this bakery with all the home baked goods that college students long for eversince the begining of their college year for them to have a heart warming feeling even if far from home.This is my first business hope you enjoy my service and products!
